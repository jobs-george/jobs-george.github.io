Website philosophy

In a world of increasing complexity and LLMs, constructing each div manually and relying less is cathartic. TODO. Reminds of how much fun it is to build stuff in plain text. And allows me to focus on the content and not worry about the appearance. This website is built in pure HTML by choice, not limitation. By relying on the simplest and most durable building block of the web, the site remains fast, accessible, and transparent. There are no unnecessary frameworks, trackers, or scripts. Just content delivered directly to the reader. Pure HTML ensures long-term stability, works on virtually any device or browser, and respects the web as an open, lightweight medium.
